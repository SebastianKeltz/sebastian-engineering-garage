Electric Dirt Bike Conversion

Overview
Electric conversion using battery, BLDC motor, controller, wiring, and throttle.
Engineering Goal
Build a reliable electric drive system and understand power delivery under real use.

Packaging, wiring organization, controller troubleshooting, safety checks, and fitment.
Testing / Results
Throttle response, voltage behavior, wiring reliability, and real-world ride checks.
What I Learned
Electrical power systems become clearer when theory is paired with measurements and inspection.
